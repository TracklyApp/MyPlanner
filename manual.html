01Getting started

MyPlanner runs straight from the planner.html file in your browser — no internet connection or account needed. Everything you enter is saved locally, in this browser, on this device.

  1. Open planner.html in your browser (double-click it in your file manager, or use Ctrl+O from an open browser window).
  2. On first launch, the app seeds a few sample items so you can see how categories and reminders look.
  3. Switch themes anytime with the 🌙 / ☀️ button in the top bar — your choice is remembered.
＊

Good to know: your data lives inside this browser. Clearing your browser's site data for local files could erase it. Use Export or Backup from the Settings menu regularly — see section 10.

04The Home dashboard

The landing page summarizes everything that matters without opening a single item.

Quick actions

➕ New Note · ✅ New Task · 🌐 Save Website · 📄 Scan Document — each opens the add-item form with the right category already selected.

Stats

The Notes, Tasks, Websites, and Favorites cards show live counts — click Notes, Websites, or Favorites to jump straight to that list.

Today, Overdue & Upcoming Reminders

Today lists everything due today. If a reminder's date has passed and it's still not marked Paid or Done, it moves into a red Overdue section instead of silently disappearing. Upcoming Reminders covers the next 5 days, grouped day by day, with a 📧 Email these button to send them all at once. All three sections can be collapsed with the ▾ arrow in their heading.

When two or more items in the same section share a category (e.g. two Health reminders), they're folded into a single row like ❤️ Health · 2 — click it to expand the dropdown and see each one.

＊

Daily reminders show every day: a reminder set to Repeat → Daily appears once per day for as many of the next 5 days as apply — not just on its stored date. Marking it Done only hides today's occurrence; tomorrow's still shows up as not done.

Pinned Notes

Pinned Notes shows anything you've pinned (📌).

Categories

Clicking a category (e.g. 📚 Books) narrows the page down to just that category, showing a horizontally-scrolling row of its items, a + Add item button preset to that category, and a ← Show all button to return.

06Reminders & Calendar

The 📅 Calendar window has three viewing modes.

Week

Seven days, Monday through Sunday.

Month

The default view — every reminder in the selected month.

Year

Every reminder in the selected year.

The ◀ ▶ arrows step to the previous/next period.

Recurring reminders

An item with Repeat set to anything other than "Does not repeat" automatically shows up in every future month/year in the calendar, on the matching day. Once a recurring reminder's date passes, the app rolls it forward to the next occurrence automatically — and clears the Amount field, and turns off Paid and Done, so you get a clean slate for the new period instead of it staying checked off forever.

07Email alerts

The app never sends email automatically in the background — it opens a pre-filled draft in your mail app, which you send yourself.

  1. From ⚙️ Settings → 📧 Set my email, enter your address — it's saved locally and auto-fills the "To" field from then on.
  2. Turn on the 📧 Email alert switch for any item you want included in bulk sends.
  3. Use the 📧 button next to a single reminder, or 📧 Email these on Home for everything due in the next 5 days.
＊

Making Gmail the default: if mailto: links don't open Gmail, visit mail.google.com in Chrome and allow "Allow Gmail to open all email links" from the address bar, then choose Chrome when Windows asks which app should handle it.

08Custom categories

Beyond the built-in categories (Websites, Books, Emails & Contacts, Ideas & Notes) and the starter set (Personal, Work, Finance, Ideas, Recipes, Travel, Health), you can create your own.

Manage them from ⚙️ Settings → 🏷️ Manage categories — this works the same on desktop and mobile:

  • Add — + Add category at the bottom of the list, then pick a name and an icon.
  • Rename / delete — every custom category has ✎ and 🗑 buttons next to it (built-in categories are marked "built-in" and can't be removed).
  • Reorder (desktop sidebar only) — drag a category over another to change its position; the order is remembered on reload.

Deleting a category automatically moves its items into "Ideas & Notes" — nothing is lost.

09Trash

Deleting an item isn't immediately permanent.

Deleted items land in 🗑️ Trash, where you can ♻️ Restore them or delete them for good. Anything left in Trash for more than 30 days is cleared automatically.

10Export, import & backup

All of these live in the ⚙️ Settings menu.

OptionWhat it does
⬇️ ExportDownloads every item as a .json file — a full backup.
🗓️ Export .icsDownloads your reminders as a calendar file, importable into Google Calendar, Outlook, or Apple Calendar (recurring ones included).
💾 BackupSaves a snapshot inside this browser, separate from your live data.
⬆️ ImportLoads a previously exported .json file — replaces your current items.
♻️ Reset demo dataClears everything and reloads the original sample items.

11Quick tips

  • Drag a card straight onto a category in the sidebar to re-file it, without opening it.
  • From Home, clicking a category narrows the page down to just that category's items — handy for bills, installments, or websites.
  • The Amount field is free text — write "315.25 RON", "$40", or whatever format you prefer.
  • Anything with a 🔁 icon next to its date is a recurring reminder — check in periodically that the amount is up to date.
